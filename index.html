Welcome to Kampong Space!

We are a team of individuals passionate in creating a hybrid co-working space for everyone.
Located in the HDB void decks, Kampong Space is the “third space” outside the central office/home that marries the best of both environments.
It keeps the employees close to home, fosters societal cohesion amongst neighbours and reduces fixed asset cost to employers.


In the post-COVID world, we envision Kampong Space to be an asset-light Public-Private Partnership, with Kampong Space designing and managing the co-working space which is built and wholly owned by the government.
